Our Story

This organization began in November 2021. We were thinking that it was a real pity there weren't enough activities in our community, so we got a great idea: we decided to create some ourselves! From the community, for the community. Since then, we have been working relentlessly to make this event possible, and hope, in spite of Covid-19 restrictions that it will take place.
